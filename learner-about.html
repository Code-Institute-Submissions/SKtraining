About SK Training

Hello and welcome! Would you like to be able to speak Dutch with your neighbors or the other parents at the playground? Or would you like to learn how to speak and write professional Dutch for your work? Or perhaps you have to take the Inburgeringsexamen or the Staatsexamen? SK Training can help you! We provide Dutch courses tailored to your needs, either with a private teacher or in a small group, in our own classroom or in your home. Learn more about our courses here.

About Sanne Kroon

My name is Sanne Kroon. I founded SK Training in 2017. I have a background in linguistics in Spanish, and am a certified NT2 teacher. Teaching is my passion, because I love witnessing the steps my students take during their learning process, and looking back at their successes and saying: “Look at that! You did that!” Having lived in France, and having learnt Spanish in a classroom, I know what it feels like when you actually have to talk to a native speaker. You freeze and lose all your confidence. Believe me, I’ve been there. But I’ve overcome it.

My specialty in practice-oriented training, which means that I’ll teach you Dutch that’s relevant for YOUR life. It means we will practice in class but I will give you homework to practice with your Dutch partner, children, friends, colleagues, or perhaps even random people in the streets. I’ll give you the tools, but in the end: you have to do it! And who knows, maybe you’ll make some new Dutch friends. Please have a look at the courses we offer. And if you’re as excited as I am, then fill out the contact form to sign up for a free intake and cup of coffee.
